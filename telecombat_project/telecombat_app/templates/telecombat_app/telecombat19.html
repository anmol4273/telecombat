TELECOMBAT 2019

"Projects we have completed demonstrate what we know-future projects decide what we will learn"

About the Contest

The Department of Telecommunications Engineering, BMSCE,under IEEE Student chapter is organising a national level project contest “TELECOMBAT”,in an attempt to bring together many young engineering minds to demonstrate innovation and technology trends through their projects.

Important Details

  • Open for TE,EC,EE,ML and E&I Departments

  • Team Size:2 to 4 members

  • There are three tracks:

  • Track A.Hardware projects without Processors

  • Track B.Hardware projects with Processors

  • Track C.Projects based on Simulation tools

  • Best 2 projects in each track,take home CASH PRIZES upto 25,000/- per track.

  • Last date to submit the abstract of project: 1-May-2019

  • Fee:

    • Rs.600/- per team
    • Rs.500/- per team for IEEE Members

Date:11-May-2019

Venue:Dept. of Telecommunication,BMSCE
